About Me

I'm a self-taught developer, based in Stellenbosch, looking to break out professionally into the world of Software Development. Physicist by education, I am wired to find simple yet elegant solutions to complex problems and I see every challenge as an opportunity to learn and grow.

While working as a programmer for an industrial laser-cutting company, I discovered Web Development and simply had to learn more. I enjoy learning about various programming languages (I've dabbled in a few), but I find myself particularly interested in front-end web technologies.

When I'm not coding or designing, you'll find me dancing, with or without music 😎
