About me

I'm a self-taught web developer/programmer, based in Stellenbosch, looking to break out professionally into the world of Information Technology. Physicist by education, I enjoy finding elegant solutions to complex problems.

While working as a programmer for an industrial laser-cutting company, I discovered web development and I simply had to learn more! I enjoy learning about various programming languages (I've dabbled in a few), but I find myself particularly interested in front-end web technologies.

When I'm not coding or designing, you'll find me dancing, with or without music ;-).
